TRIBUTE TO AN ICON: CHIEF SANI ALENI

EARLY LIFE:

Chief Aleni Sani was Born into the family of Aleni at Okpudu Ogene Autonomous Community.

He was a peasant farmer and rose to become the onu 1 of Ogene chiefdom.

REIGN AND COMMUNITY SERVICE:

Throughout His life and reign, Chielf Aleni was a beacon of hope and progress.

His commitment to public service was unwavering, and he consistently championed the cause of the underprivelege

and numerous initiatives that folster development and uplifted many from poverty.

QUOTES:

"If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together."
"I alone can not change the world, but i can cast a stone acrose the waters to create many ripples."
"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others."

CONCLUSION:

Rest in peace, Chief Sani Aleni. Your life was a gift to us all, and your memory will forever remain a guiding light.
